About Me

Hello! I am Anup Pudasaini, an agriculture graduate from Tribhuvan University, IAAS Lamjung Campus, hailing from the vibrant city of Kathmandu. I am deeply passionate about sustainable agriculture, integrating cutting-edge technology, and empowering communities to modernize farming practices in Nepal. My mission is to bridge traditional farming with modern innovations, ensuring a prosperous and sustainable future for our agricultural sector.
