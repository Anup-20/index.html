About Me

Hello! I am Anup Pudasaini, an agriculture graduate from Tribhuvan University, IAAS Lamjung Campus, and a passionate advocate for sustainable agricultural practices. I have a keen interest in integrating technology and community-driven initiatives to modernize agriculture and uplift farming communities in Nepal. I am based in Kathmandu, Nepal, where I actively contribute to various projects and research activities.

Professional Journey

Over the years, I have taken on significant leadership roles and implemented impactful programs:

  • Former President: Youth Thinkers' Society - Lamjung (2023-2024).
  • Program Coordinator: Agri Mappers - Lamjung Chapter (2020-2023).
  • Organizer of Model United Nations (MUN), fostering leadership and global awareness among youth.

Research & Academic Highlights

My research focuses on sustainable soil management, specifically integrating Arbuscular Mycorrhizal Fungi (AMF) with phosphorus in potato cultivation. Funded by NAST and the Ministry of Forest and Environment, my work supports environment-based adaptation strategies in agriculture.

Key Initiatives

  • Suicide Awareness Training: Organized in collaboration with KOSHISH Nepal to address mental health issues.
  • Agri Talk Series: A panel discussion engaging farmers, professors, local stakeholders, and researchers to address agricultural challenges.
  • School MUN: Empowered children with problem-solving and leadership skills through simulated United Nations sessions.
  • Collaborated with US Embassy Nepal and FAO Nepal to conduct impactful programs focusing on agriculture and youth development.

Current Initiatives

As the leader of the Digital Farmer Literacy Program, I am actively educating farmers on utilizing digital tools to improve productivity, embrace modern agricultural practices, and make informed decisions for sustainable farming.

Vision

My vision is to revolutionize Nepal's agricultural sector by integrating modern technologies, fostering collaborations, and empowering farming communities with knowledge and sustainable practices.
