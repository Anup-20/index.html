Research

Our research focuses on understanding the role of Arbuscular Mycorrhizal Fungi (AMF) and phosphorus in improving soil nutrient availability for potatoes. By exploring the interplay between these factors, we aim to enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability.

Key projects also include climate-resilient farming strategies and the potential of biochar in soil health improvement.

Biochar

Biochar is a game-changing tool in sustainable agriculture, offering numerous benefits such as improved soil fertility, carbon sequestration, and enhanced crop yields. We explore its applications in modern farming systems and its impact on global food security.
